4.3 Installment Calculation and Cost
----------------------------------------
Installment Fee Calculation:

Example 1: Purchase phone NT$30,000, 12 months
Handling fee: 30,000 × 7.5% = NT$2,250
Total payment: 30,000 + 2,250 = NT$32,250
Monthly payment: 32,250 ÷ 12 = NT$2,687

Example 2: Purchase appliance NT$50,000, zero-interest 6 months
Handling fee: NT$0 (borne by merchant)
Total payment: NT$50,000
Monthly payment: 50,000 ÷ 6 = NT$8,333

Example 3: Promotional installment NT$100,000, handling fee 0.3%/month, 12 months
Handling fee: 100,000 × 0.3% × 12 = NT$3,600
Total payment: 100,000 + 3,600 = NT$103,600
Monthly payment: 103,600 ÷ 12 = NT$8,633

Installment Calculation Tools:
- Website installment calculator
- App built-in calculation function
- Customer service hotline calculation
- Installment comparison table reference

Early Repayment:
- Can repay in full early
- Unexpired handling fees not refunded
- Only need to pay principal balance
- Must apply by phone for early settlement

4.4 Installment Considerations
----------------------------------------
Installment Selection Recommendations:

Prioritize Zero-Interest:
- Handling fee borne by merchant
- Can save large amount of fees
- Common for 3C, home appliances

Assess Affordability:
- Monthly installment payments not exceed 30% of monthly income
- Reserve emergency funds
- Avoid multiple installments simultaneously

Compare Installment Costs:
- Compare installment rates across banks
- Consider cash purchase discounts
- Calculate effective interest rate

Common Installment Questions:

Q: Does installment affect credit score?
A: Normal payments don't affect, can even improve credit. But too many installments may affect loan limits.

Q: Can I repay early?
A: Yes, but handling fees not refunded.

Q: Can I adjust period during installment?
A: No, must settle and re-apply for new installment.

Q: What if I'm overdue?
A: Late fees and revolving interest will be charged, affecting credit score.

7.2 Fraud Handling Process
----------------------------------------
Discovering card fraud:

Emergency Handling (Immediate):
1. Call loss reporting hotline: 02-8888-9999
2. Select "Credit Card Loss Reporting"
3. Provide card number or ID number
4. Confirm loss reporting time point
5. Inquire about recent transaction details

Post-Loss Reporting Rights:
- Fraud within 24 hours before loss reporting: Cardholder maximum NT$3,000 responsibility
- Fraud over 24 hours before loss reporting: Responsibility determined by investigation results
- Fraud after loss reporting: Bank bears full amount

Exemption Conditions:
Cardholder can be exempt from fraud amount if:
□ Fulfilled duty of care
□ Immediately reported after loss reporting
□ Cooperated with investigation providing information
□ Signature forged (handwriting verification)

Responsible Situations:
Cardholder must bear fraud amount if:
- Delivered card and password to others
- Intentional or gross negligence
- Conspired with fraudster
- Did not report loss within reasonable time

Fraud Investigation:
Bank investigation items:
- Review surveillance footage
- Compare signature handwriting
- IP address tracking (online transactions)
- Review transaction slips
- Inquire merchant

Cardholder Cooperation:
- Fill out fraud declaration form
- Explain card storage situation
- Provide alibi evidence
- Police report (for larger amounts)

New Card Replacement:
- Receive new card 3-5 business days after loss reporting
- Card number changes (security consideration)
- Original auto-payments must be reset
- Installment payments transfer to new card

7.3 Transaction Dispute Handling
----------------------------------------
Common Transaction Disputes:

Dispute Type 1: Product Not Received
Handling Method:
1. First contact merchant to confirm shipping
2. Check logistics progress
3. If merchant cannot handle, complain to bank
4. Provide order records, conversation records
5. Bank assists in claiming from merchant

Dispute Type 2: Product Defects
Handling Method:
1. Return within 7-day appreciation period per Consumer Protection Act
2. Complain to bank only if merchant refuses return
3. Provide defect photos, videos
4. Bank freezes transaction amount
5. Coordinate refund or exchange

Dispute Type 3: Duplicate Charges
Handling Method:
1. Immediately contact bank
2. Provide transaction proof (only spent once)
3. Bank contacts merchant for confirmation
4. Overcharged amount refunded

Dispute Type 4: Amount Mismatch
Handling Method:
1. Check slip amount
2. Confirm if includes service fee, tip
3. Verify exchange rate calculation (overseas spending)
4. Confirm with merchant or bank

Dispute Type 5: Subscription Deduction
Handling Method:
1. Confirm if subscription service exists
2. Check if auto-converted to paid after trial period
3. Contact service provider to cancel subscription
4. Apply to bank to cancel auto-deduction

Deduction Dispute Complaint:
Prepare Documents:
□ Transaction dispute application form
□ Credit card slip
□ Order or transaction records
□ Communication records with merchant
□ Product defect proof (photos, videos)
□ Logistics records

Submission Time Limit:
- Submit before billing due date
- Or within 60 days of discovering dispute
- Overdue considered acceptance of charges

Processing Time Limit:
- Bank acceptance: 3 business days
- Investigation period: 30-60 days
- International card organization coordination: Maximum 120 days

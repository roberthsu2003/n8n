Product Name: SonicCloud 9 Wireless Headphones
Model: SC-900
Type: Over-Ear Active Noise Cancelling (ANC)

1. Key Features
- Hybrid ANC technology reduces ambient noise by up to 35dB.
- Transparency Mode allows you to hear surroundings without removing headphones.
- Multi-point connection: Connect to two devices simultaneously.

2. Battery & Charging
- Battery Life (ANC On): 30 hours
- Battery Life (ANC Off): 45 hours
- Charging Time: 2 hours for a full charge via USB-C.
- Quick Charge: 10 minutes of charging provides 3 hours of playback.

3. Controls (Touch Pad on Right Ear Cup)
- Play/Pause: Double tap.
- Volume Up: Swipe up.
- Volume Down: Swipe down.
- Next Track: Swipe forward.
- Toggle ANC/Transparency: Long press for 2 seconds.

4. LED Indicator Status
- Flashing Red/Blue: Pairing mode.
- Solid Blue: Connected.
- Flashing Red: Low battery (below 10%).
- Solid Green: Fully charged.

5. Warranty
- Standard Warranty: 1 year from purchase date.
- Water Resistance: IPX4 (Sweat resistant, do not submerge).

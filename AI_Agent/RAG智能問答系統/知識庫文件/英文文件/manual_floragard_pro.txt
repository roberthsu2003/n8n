Product Name: FloraGard Pro
Model: FG-PRO-V2
Purpose: Soil moisture and sunlight monitor for indoor plants.

1. Installation
Insert the FloraGard Pro probe into the soil about 3 to 4 inches deep. Ensure the light sensor on the top is not covered by leaves.

2. App Setup
Download the "FloraLink" app from the App Store or Google Play. Turn on Bluetooth on your phone and tap "Add Device."

3. Sensor Readings
- Moisture Range: 0% (Dry) to 100% (Wet)
- Sunlight Intensity: 0 to 50,000 Lux
- Temperature Range: -10°C to 50°C
- Battery: Uses one CR2032 coin battery (lasts approx. 1 year).

4. Light Status Definitions
- Green Flash: Soil moisture is optimal.
- Yellow Flash: Soil is getting dry.
- Red Flash: Critical moisture level! Water immediately.
- Blue Flash: Firmware updating.

5. Common Questions
Q: Why is the device offline?
A: The FloraGard Pro uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). You must be within 10 meters of the device to sync data.

Q: Can I use it outdoors?
A: Yes, but it is not fully waterproof. Do not expose to heavy rain.

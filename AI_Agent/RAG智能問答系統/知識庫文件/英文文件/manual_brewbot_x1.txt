Product Name: BrewBot X1 Smart Coffee Maker
Model: BB-X1-2024
Manufacturer: FutureBrew Inc.

1. Product Overview
The BrewBot X1 is a WiFi-enabled coffee maker designed for home use. It features a 1.5L water tank and supports voice control via Alexa and Google Assistant.

2. Technical Specifications
- Power: 1200W
- Voltage: 110V-220V
- Water Tank Capacity: 1.5 Liters
- Brewing Temperature: 92°C to 96°C (Adjustable via App)
- Connectivity: 2.4GHz WiFi only (5GHz not supported)

3. Operation Instructions
- Power On: Press the circular button on the front panel once.
- Pairing Mode: Hold the power button for 5 seconds until the LED blinks blue.
- Factory Reset: Hold the power button and the "Brew" button simultaneously for 10 seconds.

4. Maintenance
- Descaling: The "Clean" light will turn orange after 300 brew cycles. Please use the FutureBrew Descaling Solution.
- Filter: Replace the charcoal water filter every 2 months.

5. Troubleshooting (Error Codes)
- Error E01: Water tank is empty. Please refill.
- Error E02: Coffee grinder jammed. Please clean the bean hopper.
- Error E03: Overheating protection triggered. Unplug for 30 minutes.
- Error E04: WiFi connection lost. Check your router.
